Security

We know from experience that providing the best security is about having the right people for each specific job environment. At radar, we meet the needs of every job by using our strategic staffing model. This means that we determine the specific mix of skills and attributes needed for each position, so you can feel confident knowing you are getting the best person for the job. Radar offers a variety of guard services as a way to customize a security solution that fits your unique environment and budget.

We believe in providing our clients with excellent customer service along with a specialised security service. We allocate a dedicated client manager who is wholly responsible for ensuring the highest level of service delivery is provided to your site. They will conduct regular meetings with your team to ensure you are receiving the full benefit of trusting us to secure your site and how we can help you further.

HOUSEMAID

JOBS HUNT RESOURCING COMPANY is equip with well trained and competent recruitment officers,marketing staff and interviewers that conduct Special Recruitment Activities (SRAs), Job Fairs and Interviews in different provinces and regions of the KENYA to look for workers and/or applicants that are highly skilled, experienced, and has a good working attitude and personal character for our foreign principals and/or clients we provide housemaids with :

  • • Excellent/Fluent written and spoken English
  • • Trained and qualified from government school
  • • Skilled with thinking on their feet
  • • Keep calm under pressure
  • • Posses a responsible and mature nature
  • • Qualified in all house cleaning tasks and follow the weekly or daily work plan

CLEANERS

  • • A Cleaner is responsible for all basic cleaning in and around the facility or office building. This can entail dusting, mopping, sweeping, vacuuming, and cleaning smudges off windows and doors. Ensuring restrooms are cleaned, sanitized, and restocked.
